Hotel Management System - Internal Admin Module
DBMS Project | B.Tech Information Technology | NSUT Delhi

A comprehensive database-driven hotel management system designed for internal staff/attendants to efficiently manage guest operations, room inventory, bookings, payments, and billing from the admin 
perspective.

#Project Overview
The Internal Hotel Management System is a DBMS project developed as part of the B.Tech Information Technology curriculum at Netaji Subhas University of Technology (NSUT), Delhi. 
This system is designed exclusively for internal use by hotel staff and administrative personnel to streamline daily operations.

#Purpose
Provide a unified platform for hotel staff to:
- Register and manage guest information
- Track room availability and status in real-time
- Create and manage reservations and bookings
- Process payments through multiple methods
- Generate itemized bills automatically
- Manage additional room services
- Access reports and analytics
- Control staff roles and permissions

#Scope
Target Users: Hotel receptionists, attendants, managers, and administrative staff
Access Level: Internal staff only (role-based access control)
Environment: On-premises hotel management system
Scale: Support for small to medium-sized hotels 

#Features
Core Functionality
1. Guest Management
- Guest Registration: Comprehensive registration form with ID proof verification
- Guest Profile: Store and retrieve guest information including contact details, address, ID proof
- Guest Search: Quick search functionality with multiple filter options
- Guest History: Track all bookings and transactions for each guest

2. Room Management
- Room Inventory: Complete database of all hotel rooms with details
- Room Categorization: Classify rooms by type (Single, Double, Suite, Deluxe)
- Pricing Management: Set and manage pricing for different room types
- Real-Time Availability: Display current room status (Available, Occupied, Reserved, Under Maintenance)
- Room Capacity: Track occupancy limits per room
- Room Features: Document amenities and special features for each room

3. Booking Management
- Create Bookings: Register new guest reservations with check-in/check-out dates
- Booking Confirmation: Automatic confirmation with unique booking ID
- Modify Bookings: Edit booking details (dates, room type, guest info)
- Booking History: View all past and current bookings
- Number of Guests: Track guest count per booking

4. Payment Processing
- Multiple Payment Methods: Accept Cash, Cards, UPI
- Payment Recording: Log all payment transactions with timestamp
- Payment Status: Track payment completion, pending, failed, or refunded status
- Transaction ID: Maintain transaction references for verification
- Payment History: Complete audit trail of all payments

5. Billing System
- Automated Bill Generation: Calculate bills based on room charges and services
- Room Charges: Compute charges based on room type, number of nights
- Service Charges: Include additional services (room service, laundry, etc.)
- Tax Calculation: Automatic GST application (12%)
- Bill Details: Itemized breakdown of all charges

6. Room Services
- Service Requests: Record room service requests (food, laundry, housekeeping, etc.)
- Service Tracking: Monitor service request status
- Service Pricing: Maintain pricing for different services
- Service History: Track all services provided to guests

7. Staff Management
- Employee Records: Maintain complete staff information
- Role Assignment: Assign roles (Manager, Receptionist, Housekeeping, Maintenance, Admin)
- Department Management: Organize staff by departments
- Access Control: Role-based permission management

8. Reports & Analytics
- Occupancy Reports: Real-time occupancy rates and trends
- Revenue Reports: Daily, weekly, monthly revenue summaries
- Payment Reports: Summary of payment methods and amounts
